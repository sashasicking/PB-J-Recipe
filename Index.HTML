Poutine recipe

Learn how to make easy and authentic Poutine

Ingredients

  • 1 quart of vegetable oil frying
  • canned beef gravy
  • 5 medium potatoes, cut into fries
  • 2 cups of cheese curds

instructions

  1. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 185 degrees c.
  2. While the oil is heating, begin to warm gravy
  3. Place fries into oil, and cook until light brown.
  4. Place fries on a serving platter, sprinkle cheese over them
  5. Pour hot gravy evenly around the fries and cheese then enjoy
